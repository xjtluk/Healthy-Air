App Showcase

Main Dashboard

Our intuitive dashboard provides at-a-glance information about current air quality. The color-coded interface makes it easy to understand pollution levels:

  • Good (0-50): Safe for everyone
  • Moderate (51-100): Acceptable but may affect sensitive groups
  • Unhealthy (101-150): Everyone may experience health effects
  • Dangerous (151+): Health warnings, everyone may experience serious effects

Detailed Pollutant Information

Tap on any pollutant card to access comprehensive information about:

  • What the pollutant is and its sources
  • Health effects and risks at different levels
  • Recommended protective measures
  • Contextual advice based on current readings

This educational feature helps users understand exactly what they're breathing and how to protect themselves.

Data Collection & Management

What We Collect

Air Quality Data

We fetch comprehensive air quality information from the AQICN API, including AQI values, pollutant concentrations (PM2.5, PM10, O₃, NO₂, CO, etc.), and the dominant pollutant.

Location Data

With your permission, we use device GPS to determine your current coordinates and convert them into city names through reverse geocoding.

User Preferences

We save your notification settings, favorite cities, and update frequency preferences to personalize your experience.

How We Handle Your Data

Security First

All user data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore with industry-standard encryption and authentication protocols.

Privacy Focused

We only collect what's necessary to provide our service. Your location data is never shared with third parties and is only used to fetch relevant air quality information.

Real-time Updates

Our state management system ensures your UI always reflects the latest data while maintaining performance and battery efficiency.

Data Collection
